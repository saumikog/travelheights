Meet Your Guide

Hello! I'm Narendra Kumar Verma, a certified tourist guide based in Agra. With over 13 years of experience, I offer insightful, safe, and memorable tours to both local and international visitors.

Not many people pursue a career in the same industry they once dreamed of. But I am proud to say that I am one of those few who have turned their passion into a profession—with immense joy, dedication, and enthusiasm.

I have always seen tourism as a form of social service. Even today, as a professional, I strive to offer the best possible service to my guests from all around the world. I believe true service should never be compromised—regardless of the tour package provided by any travel agent.

Please note, I am not charging anything extra from your travel agent. But it’s important to understand that a tour guide is the ambassador of a country. A guide ensures your safety, comfort, and satisfaction during your journey—not just by showing you places, but by being available to share knowledge, assist in every way possible, and make your experience memorable.

I speak English and Hindi fluently and am passionate about sharing the cultural and historical heritage of Agra. My license is certified by the Indian Government, ensuring you a professional and trustworthy experience.
