Discover the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It's renowned for its stunning architecture, considered a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic style, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The complex includes the main mausoleum, a mosque, a guest house, and gardens, all situated on the banks of the Yamuna River.

Explore the Majestic Agra Fort

The Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of the most significant monuments in India. Built primarily in red sandstone by Emperor Akbar, the fort served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors until 1638. Within its walls lie stunning palaces, grand halls, and exquisite architecture reflecting centuries of history and power.
