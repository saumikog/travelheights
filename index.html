The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It's renowned for its stunning architecture, considered a masterpiece of Indo-Islamic style, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The complex includes the main mausoleum, a mosque, a guest house, and gardens, all situated on the banks of the Yamuna River.
